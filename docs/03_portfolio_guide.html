🎯 The Story Your Portfolio Must Tell

The 5-Part Narrative Arc

  • 1. Problem: "TeleCalifornia has a 26.5% churn rate — above industry average. Their best customers are leaving."
  • 2. Data: "I joined 3 datasets (7,043 customers, population data, data dictionary), handled all missing values with business logic — no blind drops."
  • 3. Discovery: "77% of customers churn in their first 6 months. Fiber Optic users — the highest-paying — churn at 42%. Month-to-Month contracts are 20× riskier than Two-Year."
  • 4. Prediction: "My Random Forest model achieves AUC = 0.9251. It identified 1,511 Critical Risk customers holding $13.6M in annual revenue exposure."
  • 5. Action: "Contract upgrade campaigns + onboarding redesign can save $648K/year at a base-case churn reduction of 8 points."
💡 Recruiter Psychology: They spend 90 seconds on a portfolio. Your README's first 3 lines must answer: What problem? What result? What tools? Everything else is proof.
