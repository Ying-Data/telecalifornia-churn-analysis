Strategic Retention Playbook

Six retention programs ranked by impact and feasibility. Conservative to optimistic ROI scenarios modelled against a $150K annual programme cost.

1 · Contract Upgrade Campaign — Month-to-Month → Annual
Target 1.869 M-t-M churners with personalised upgrade offers (e.g., 2 months free or device discount). A 20% conversion converts 374 customers to 1-year contracts. Churn rate drops from 51,7% → ~30%.
$290K–$430K/yr
2 · Onboarding Excellence Programme — First 6 Months
77,2% churn in months 0–6 signals broken onboarding. Implement a 90-day check-in sequence, proactive service calls at day 30, and a dedicated customer success rep for Fiber customers.
$180K–$250K/yr
3 · Fiber Optic Retention Programme
Fiber users churn at 42,1% despite paying ~$90+/month. Root cause: competitors offering better speeds/devices. Response: guaranteed speed SLA, annual device refresh, and loyalty pricing for 2-year Fiber contracts.
$210K–$350K/yr
4 · Pause Offer E Immediately — 67,6% Churn Rate
Offer E has a catastrophic 67,6% churn rate vs. 6,7% for Offer A. This offer may be attracting price-sensitive, low-loyalty customers. Audit the offer structure and pause new enrollments pending review.
$95K–$140K/yr
5 · Competitive Intelligence & Device Trade-In Programme
45% of churn cites "competitor made a better offer." Build a real-time competitive monitoring dashboard and launch an annual device upgrade programme for customers on contracts 18+ months.
$130K–$200K/yr
6 · Activate ML Predictions in CRM
Deploy the churn probability score (Churn_Probability column) directly into Salesforce or equivalent CRM. Set automated alerts when Critical Risk scores exceed 0,70, triggering a retention workflow.
$100K–$180K/yr

Appendix — Data Quality Notes

Missing values: all explained by business logic. No internet service → no online security/backup/streaming fields (blank = "Not applicable", not missing). 120 customers with negative Monthly Charge treated as billing credits and clipped to zero. All 7.043 ZIP codes matched population data — 100% join rate.

Appendix — Model Methodology

Algorithm: Random Forest (n_estimators=200, max_depth=12, class_weight='balanced'). Train/test: 80/20 stratified split. Validation: 5-fold cross-validation. Class imbalance handled via balanced class weights. Threshold: 0,50 for binary classification, 0,70 for Critical Risk tier.
